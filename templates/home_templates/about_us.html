About Us

This is Jeremy's Capstone Project!

We had to make a web application with business logic.

My project runs simulations to represent a day of customers making orders at an ice cream store!




I collect the data by inserting it into a database and I used SQLite for my DB.

I then use that data to display the results, make charts, use AI, and make aggregate calculations.

I used the SUM function, AVG function, and the CEIL functions in some of my queries to the DB.




I used Matplotlib to make the charts that were rendered by my templates.

I used Ski Kit Learning for the AI and I displayed the Mean Squared Error to see how accurate it was.
